About Me

I like telling stories. Or rather, I have to. You see, my six-year-old daughter doesn't sleep until I tell her a new bedtime story. A new story every night!

At heart, all of us are like a six-year-old. We love our new stories but we also love to hold the older stories which are dear to us, close to our hearts, look at them time-to-time and relive them. Maybe even share them with our loved ones or even the whole world.

Every photo not only tells a story, but it also has a few of its own and we are the characters in all those stories.

And I like telling stories!
